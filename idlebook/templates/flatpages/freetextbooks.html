What is Idlebook?

Avoid the long buyback lines and low buyback price, and make money with just a few clicks on your computer. Idlebook is a peer to peer way to buy, rent and sell your books. You can find out what books your friends have, and lend your books to others while making extra bucks. Find out more at how it works.

Who are we?

Idlebook was founded by former UW students Sean Ren from Computer Science and Tommy Yang from Foster School of Business. We saw a need for cheap and accessible peer to peer textbook options for college students and we're trying to make it easy.
